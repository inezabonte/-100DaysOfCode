Kobe Bryant

Here is a timeline of Kobe...

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia
  • 1996 - At 17 years old he signed a three year rookie contract totalling to $3.5 million.
  • 1997 - Youngest dunk champion at the age of 18
  • 1998 - Signed a $70 million contract extension for six years
  • 2002 - He claimed his first All-Star MVP trophy after a 31-point performance in Philadelphia. All-NBA Defensive team again,
    Bryant was also named to the All-NBA First Team for the first time in his career.
  • 2004 - Bryant declined an offer to sign with the Los Angeles Clippers and re-signed with the Lakers, on a seven-year contract
  • 2005 - Bryant scored 62 points in three quarters against the Dallas Mavericks. Entering the fourth quarter,

  • 2015 - Bryant announced via The Players' Tribune that he would be retiring at the end of the season
  • 2016 - Bryant and his business partner Jeff Stibel launched Bryant-Stibel, a venture capital firm
